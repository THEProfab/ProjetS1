Objectives of the internship

You will find on this page the general objectives of the internship, and the ones more specific to the foreign internship.

More informations on the Informatic Curriculum

What are the goals of internships ?

The internship is the last step in a student's formation. This is the reason why it is really important and must not be neglected.
Internships allows the students to become aware of the socio-professional environment and to perceive the reality of the profession in everyday life. Indeed, they will work with professionnals for the entire internship. They could ask for tips, execute and improve the knowledge gained at the University Institute of Technology. Moreover, during this internship, the students will see if this profession and this everyday life really please them. This will let them know if they still want to continue on this way or not.


In what way could a foreign experience be beneficial ?

Carrying out a foreign internship offers new really interesting possibilities for the students.
By leaving their routine and their comfort areas, they became firstl, more autonomus and more mature because they will have to manage on their own in a town, a country that they maybe don't know. By working daily with natives of the country, they will quickly learn a new language or improve their level in it. Students can also learn technical and specific vocabulary about the tasks that they will have to do by being close to professors, professionals or others students. Moreover, students will discover the education system of the country they are studying in. This one could be different than the one used in France. Therefore, it will be necessary that they adapt to this new system if they want to succeed. In any case, a foreign internship could only be profitable for students, who can make mention of it on their C.V., which adds value to it compared to the students who did not do a foreign internship.
